<p><strong>The Medical Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lithium ion (Li-ion) battery</li><li>Nickel cadmium (Ni-Cd) battery</li><li>Nickel metal hydride (Nimh) battery</li><li>Alkaline-manganese battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical batteries market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications in healthcare devices. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries offer high energy density and long lifespan, ideal for portable equipment. Nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are reliable but less common due to environmental concerns. Nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) batteries provide a balance of capacity and safety, often used in handheld devices. Alkaline-manganese batteries, known for affordability and ease of use, are typically found in low-drain devices such as remote controls and basic medical tools.</p></p>

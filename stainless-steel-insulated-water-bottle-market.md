<p><ul><li>Non-Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup</li><li>Vacuum Stainless Steel Thermos Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel insulated water bottle market is divided into two main types: non-vacuum stainless steel thermos cups and vacuum stainless steel thermos cups. Non-vacuum thermos cups provide insulation through double-wall steel construction but do not create a vacuum seal, resulting in less effective temperature retention. In contrast, vacuum stainless steel thermos cups incorporate a vacuum between walls, significantly enhancing thermal insulation, keeping beverages hot or cold for extended periods, making them more popular among consumers seeking superior performance.</p></p>

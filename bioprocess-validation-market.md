<p><p>The bioprocess validation market encompasses several critical segments: Extractables and Leachables (E&L), Integrity Testing, and Microbiology Testing. E&L ensures that materials in contact with biologics do not release harmful substances, safeguarding product safety. Integrity Testing verifies the performance of sterilization barriers, crucial for maintaining aseptic conditions. Microbiology Testing assesses the sterility and microbial load of biopharmaceutical products, ensuring compliance with health standards. Together, these segments support the production of safe and effective biopharmaceutical products.</p></p>

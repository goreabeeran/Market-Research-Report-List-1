<p><p>The fire-resistant glass market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Laminated Fire-Resistant Glass** offers enhanced safety by sandwiching resin between glass layers, which prevents shattering. </p><p>2. **Tempered Fire-Resistant Glass** is heat-treated for strength, ideal for handling thermal stress. </p><p>3. **Wired Fire-Resistant Glass** features an embedded wire mesh, improving resistance against flames and heat while maintaining visibility. </p><p>4. **Ceramic Fire-Resistant Glass** withstands high temperatures and provides superior insulation, making it suitable for fireplaces and fire-rated openings. Each type serves specific applications in fire protection.</p></p>

<p><p>The Photovoltaic System EPC market is divided into two main types: Centralized and Distributed Photovoltaic EPC. Centralized Photovoltaic EPC refers to large-scale solar power plants that generate electricity in a single location, typically connected to the grid and designed for higher energy output. In contrast, Distributed Photovoltaic EPC involves smaller, decentralized solar installations that generate power closer to the point of consumption, often residential or commercial, promoting energy independence and reducing transmission losses.</p></p>

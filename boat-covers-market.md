<p><strong>The Boat Covers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven</li><li>Non-Woven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat covers are categorized into two primary market types: woven and non-woven. Woven boat covers are made from interlaced fibers, offering durability and strength, often providing better protection against harsh weather while allowing breathability. On the other hand, non-woven boat covers are made from fibers that are bonded together, resulting in a lightweight and cost-effective option. While they may offer less durability than woven covers, they still protect against dust and debris, making them suitable for short-term use or indoor storage.</p></p>

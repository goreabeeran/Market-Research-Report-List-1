<p><strong>The Spray Foam Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray foam equipment market is categorized into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell spray foam consists of tightly packed cells that create a rigid structure, offering superior insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for exterior applications. Open cell spray foam, on the other hand, has a softer, more flexible composition that provides excellent sound absorption and is typically used for interior spaces. Each type serves distinct insulation needs, influencing market demand and application preferences.</p></p>

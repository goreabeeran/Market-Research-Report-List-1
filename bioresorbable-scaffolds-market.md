<p><p>The bioresorbable scaffolds market is primarily classified into two types: metal-based and polymer-based scaffolds. Metal-based bioresorbable scaffolds utilize materials like magnesium or iron, offering strength and structural support, and gradually dissolve in the body. In contrast, polymer-based bioresorbable scaffolds are composed of biodegradable polymers, providing flexibility and versatility in design, and are also absorbed by the body over time. Both types are used in various medical applications, particularly in cardiovascular and orthopedic treatments, promoting healing and tissue regeneration.</p></p>

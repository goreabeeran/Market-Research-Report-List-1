<p><p>The cordless curling wands market is divided into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic curling wands feature built-in mechanisms that automatically wind hair into the barrel, providing a user-friendly experience and consistent curls with minimal effort. In contrast, manual curling wands require users to wrap their hair around the barrel themselves, offering personalized styling control and versatility. Both types cater to different user preferences, with automatic wands appealing to those seeking convenience and manual wands favored for traditional styling methods.</p></p>

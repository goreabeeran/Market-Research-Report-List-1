<p><ul><li>Light Molasses</li><li>Dark Molasses</li><li>Blackstrap Molasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry molasses market is categorized into three main types: light molasses, dark molasses, and blackstrap molasses. Light molasses has a milder taste and is often used in baking and cooking. Dark molasses, richer in flavor and nutrients, is suitable for animal feed and certain food products. Blackstrap molasses, the byproduct of sugar extraction, is highly concentrated with vitamins and minerals, making it popular in health supplements and as a feed additive. Each type serves distinct culinary and agricultural purposes.</p></p>

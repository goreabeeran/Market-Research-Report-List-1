<p><p>The small rotary drilling rig market features two primary types: crawler and rubber-tired rigs. Crawler rigs are equipped with tracks, enhancing stability and mobility on uneven terrains, making them ideal for construction and foundation projects. In contrast, rubber-tired rigs use wheels, offering greater speed and flexibility on paved surfaces, which is beneficial for urban drilling applications. Both types serve specific use cases, catering to varying site conditions and operational needs within the drilling industry.</p></p>

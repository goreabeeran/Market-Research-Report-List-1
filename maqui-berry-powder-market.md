<p><strong>The Maqui Berry Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maqui berry powder is produced primarily through two drying methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly heating the berry juice to create a fine powder, preserving essential nutrients while maintaining a longer shelf life. In contrast, freeze drying preserves the berries by first freezing them and then removing moisture under a vacuum, resulting in a powder that retains more flavor and nutritional content. Both methods cater to varying market demands, balancing cost and quality for consumers.</p></p>

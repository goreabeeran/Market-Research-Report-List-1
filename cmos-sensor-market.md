<p><strong>The CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS). PPS utilizes a simple design that captures light with minimal circuitry, making it less expensive but with lower image quality and speed. In contrast, APS integrates individual transistors for each pixel, enhancing image quality, speed, and functionality, making it suitable for advanced applications like high-resolution cameras and smartphones. The APS segment is the dominant force in the growing CMOS sensor market.</p></p>

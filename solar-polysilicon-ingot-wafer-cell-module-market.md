<p><p>The Solar Polysilicon Ingot Wafer Cell Module market features two primary connection types: series and parallel. In series connection, solar cells are linked sequentially, which boosts voltage while maintaining current; this configuration optimizes energy output for larger systems. Conversely, parallel connection links cells side-by-side, enhancing current output without increasing voltage, thus improving performance in shaded conditions. These connection methods influence overall efficiency, installation designs, and compatibility in various applications within the growing solar energy market.</p></p>

<p><p>Cork brick market types can be categorized into round hole and non-round hole markets based on the design of the cork bricks. Round hole cork bricks feature a circular perforation, which can enhance insulation properties and allow for easier interlocking during construction. Non-round hole cork bricks, on the other hand, may have various shapes, offering versatility in design and application. Both types serve different needs in construction and insulation, catering to specific aesthetic and functional requirements in various building projects.</p></p>

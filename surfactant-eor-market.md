<p><p>Surfactant Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves using surfactants to improve oil extraction from reservoirs. The market is categorized mainly into anionic surfactants and other surfactants. Anionic surfactants, which carry a negative charge, are effective in reducing interfacial tension and improving oil mobility. Other surfactants include cationic, nonionic, and zwitterionic types, each offering unique properties and advantages for specific reservoir conditions. Together, these surfactants enhance oil recovery efficiency and contribute to sustainable extraction methods in the petroleum industry.</p></p>

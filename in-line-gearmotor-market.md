<p><p>The in-line gearmotor market features three main types: planetary, helical, and helical bevel. Planetary gearmotors offer high torque and compact size due to their unique design, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and efficiency. Helical gearmotors provide smooth and quiet operation with high load capacity, suitable for various industrial settings. Helical bevel gearmotors combine helical gearing with bevel gears, allowing for changes in drive direction while maintaining efficiency, making them versatile for applications with space constraints.</p></p>

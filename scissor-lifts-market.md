<p><p>The scissor lifts market consists of three primary types: hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical. Hydraulic scissor lifts use fluid pressure for smooth vertical movement, making them ideal for heavy loads. Pneumatic scissor lifts operate using compressed air, offering a lightweight option suitable for indoor applications. Mechanical scissor lifts rely on a system of gears or levers, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environments, influencing their adoption across various industries.</p></p>

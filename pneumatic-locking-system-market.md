<p><p>The pneumatic locking system market is divided into static and dynamic types. Static pneumatic locking systems are designed for fixed applications where the locking mechanism remains in place until intentional release, ensuring secure holding of objects or equipment. In contrast, dynamic pneumatic locking systems allow for movement while providing locking capabilities, typically engaging and disengaging seamlessly during operation. Both types serve different functions based on the requirements of stability and mobility in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>

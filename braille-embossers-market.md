<p><p>The Braille embossers market comprises two main types: single-sided and double-sided embossers. Single-sided Braille embossers print Braille characters on one side of the paper, offering simplicity and lower costs, suitable for basic Braille production. In contrast, double-sided Braille embossers can print Braille on both sides of the sheet, allowing for more efficient use of materials and the ability to create thicker documents. This feature is ideal for producing more complex documents that require extensive Braille formatting.</p></p>

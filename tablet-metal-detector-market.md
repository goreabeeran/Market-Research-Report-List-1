<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet metal detectors utilize various systems to ensure product safety in pharmaceutical applications. Balanced coil systems use two coils to detect metal contaminants by measuring the disruptions in electromagnetic fields, offering high sensitivity and minimal false alarms. Ferrous-in-foil systems focus on identifying ferrous metals within foil packaging. They employ specialized sensors to penetrate the foil barrier effectively, ensuring that even small metal fragments are detected, thus maintaining product integrity and compliance with safety standards. Both types enhance quality control in manufacturing processes.</p></p>

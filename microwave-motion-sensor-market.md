<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Microwave Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Radar</li><li>Doppler-effect</li><li>Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave</li><li>Ultra-wideband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave motion sensor market comprises various types, each utilizing different technologies. Pulsed radar sensors emit short bursts of microwave signals to detect movement based on the time it takes for the signal to return. Doppler-effect sensors measure frequency changes in reflected waves, indicating motion. Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (FMCW) sensors provide precise distance measurements through continuous signal modulation. Ultra-wideband (UWB) sensors use a broad spectrum of frequencies for enhanced resolution and accuracy, making them ideal for advanced applications in security and automation.</p></p>

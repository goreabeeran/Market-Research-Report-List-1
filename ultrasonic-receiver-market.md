<p><p>The ultrasonic receiver market comprises several types, including Doppler type, sound field type, and others. Doppler type receivers utilize the Doppler effect to measure changes in frequency, commonly used in flow measurements and distance sensing. Sound field type receivers detect sound waves in a specific area, focusing on acoustic pressure variations, often applied in environmental and industrial monitoring. Other types may include specialized receivers tailored for unique applications, such as medical imaging or non-destructive testing, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>

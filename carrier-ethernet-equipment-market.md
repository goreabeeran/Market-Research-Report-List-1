Local Area Network (LAN) equipment focuses on enabling high-speed communication within a limited geographical area, such as a single building or campus. Metropolitan Area Network (MAN) equipment connects multiple LANs across a city, providing efficient data transfer over medium distances. Wide Area Network (WAN) equipment facilitates connectivity over broader areas, including national or international networks, supporting extensive data transmission and diverse applications for various enterprises.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Magnetic Adjustment</li><li>Gravity Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adjustable drainage valves market includes two main types: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment. Magnetic adjustment valves utilize magnets to enable quick and precise changes in drainage flow, providing enhanced control and efficiency. In contrast, gravity adjustment valves rely on the gravitational force to regulate drainage, offering a simpler, more traditional mechanism. Both types are essential in various applications, allowing users to fine-tune drainage systems for optimal performance, prevent flooding, and manage water levels effectively.</p></p>

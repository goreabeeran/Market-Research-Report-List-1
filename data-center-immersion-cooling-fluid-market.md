<p><strong>The Data Center Immersion Cooling Fluid Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-phase Immersion Cooling</li><li>Two-phase Immersion Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data center immersion cooling fluid market is categorized into single-phase and two-phase immersion cooling applications. Single-phase immersion cooling utilizes liquids to absorb heat from electronic components, transferring it to a cooling system without phase change, ensuring steady temperatures. In contrast, two-phase immersion cooling leverages the phase change of the coolant, facilitating heat transfer as the liquid vaporizes and condenses, enhancing cooling efficiency. Both methods prioritize energy efficiency, thermal management, and reducing operational costs in data centers.</p></p>

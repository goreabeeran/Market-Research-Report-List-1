<p><p>The 4H Silicon Carbide (SiC) substrates market features primarily two types: semi-insulating and n-type. Semi-insulating 4H SiC substrates possess high resistivity and are used in high-frequency and high-power applications, ideal for power electronic devices where minimal conductivity is essential. In contrast, n-type substrates are doped to provide free electrons, enhancing conductivity and supporting applications in electronic devices that require efficient charge transport. Together, these types cater to diverse sectors like energy, automotive, and electronics, driving advancements in technology.</p></p>

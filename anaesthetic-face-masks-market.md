<p><p>Anaesthetic face masks are categorized based on their material types, each offering distinct properties. Polypropylene (PP) and polyethylene (PE) are lightweight and cost-effective but may lack flexibility. Thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) provide a softer feel while maintaining durability. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is versatile and affordable but can be less comfortable. Silicone rubber is highly flexible and durable, providing an excellent seal and comfort. Other materials may include varying blends designed for specific applications or enhanced sterility, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
